AP Trucking Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 March 2026

AP Trucking provides freight booking, trip tracking, settlement, OTP login, and admin operations tools for shippers, drivers, and operations teams.

Information we collect

  • Account data such as name, mobile number, role, and optional email.
  • Trip and load data such as pickup/drop addresses, vehicle information, and shipment progress.
  • Live and background location data while a driver is on an active trip.
  • Payment metadata required for Razorpay orders, settlements, and reconciliation.
  • Security logs such as IP address, device details, OTP attempt counts, and fraud signals.

Why we use data

  • Authenticate users with OTP and protect accounts against abuse.
  • Track active trips, calculate ETAs, and maintain shipment visibility.
  • Process payments, payouts, settlement review, and ledger history.
  • Detect fraud, investigate disputes, and comply with legal obligations.

Location data

Background location is collected only for active trip tracking, route monitoring, fraud checks, and shipment visibility. Drivers can stop tracking when a trip ends.

Third-party services

  • Twilio Verify for OTP delivery and verification.
  • Razorpay for payment order creation, payment verification, and settlement operations.
  • PostgreSQL and Redis infrastructure for secure data and session storage.
  • Sentry, if enabled, for crash monitoring.

Data retention

We retain account, transaction, trip, and audit records only as long as needed for service delivery, fraud prevention, finance reconciliation, and legal compliance.

Your choices

You can request account deletion, account export, or support by emailing support@aptrucking.in. Operational and tax records may be retained where required by law.
