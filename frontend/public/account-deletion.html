AP Trucking Account Deletion Request

Last updated: 27 March 2026

Users can request deletion of their AP Trucking account and associated non-regulatory profile data by email.

How to request deletion

  1. Email support@aptrucking.in from the mobile number or email linked to your account.
  2. Include your full name, registered mobile number, user role, and reason for deletion.
  3. Our team will verify the request and confirm completion.

What gets deleted

  • Profile access, active sessions, and OTP login access.
  • Stored preferences and non-essential operational profile data.

What may be retained

  • Trip, payment, settlement, ledger, and tax records required for legal, fraud, and finance compliance.
  • Security audit logs needed to investigate abuse or disputes.

Timeline

Deletion requests are generally processed within 7 business days after ownership verification.
